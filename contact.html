Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the information on your periodic table? +
Our periodic table data is sourced from reputable scientific databases and is regularly updated to reflect the latest discoveries and measurements from the scientific community.
Can I use your periodic table for educational purposes? +
Absolutely! Our website is designed to be an educational resource. Teachers and students are encouraged to use our interactive periodic table in classrooms and for learning activities.
Do you have mobile apps available? +
Currently, we only have a web-based application that is fully responsive and works well on mobile devices. We are exploring the development of dedicated mobile apps in the future.
How often is new element information added? +
We monitor scientific publications and updates from organizations like IUPAC. When new elements are officially recognized or when significant updates to element properties are published, we update our database accordingly.
Can I contribute to or suggest improvements for the website? +
Yes! We welcome feedback and suggestions from our users. Please use the contact form above to share your ideas with us.
